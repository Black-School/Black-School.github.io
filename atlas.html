Execution flow

How Atlas Works

01

Scan the Universe

Every morning at 9:15 AM, Atlas downloads fresh data for all 50 Nifty stocks and rebuilds the day’s opportunity set.

02

Run Five Alpha Signals

Each stock is scored by five uncorrelated quantitative factors so the ranking engine does not depend on one market regime.

03

AI Validation

Top-ranked stocks go through an LLM for fundamental analysis before the final shortlist is approved for execution.

04

Execute & Protect

Orders fire automatically via broker API with trailing stops, portfolio constraints, and a daily loss cap guarding the book.

Alpha model

The Five Alpha Signals

α₁ Volume-Weighted Momentum

+21.2%/yr • Weight 3.0

Detects stocks where price rises are backed by increasing volume, favoring momentum with real participation behind it.

α₂ Keltner Channel Breakout

+19.5%/yr • Weight 3.0

Catches explosive moves when price breaks above the Keltner channel and expands out of compressed trading ranges.

α₃ MA Momentum

+16.1%/yr • Weight 2.5

Trend-following signal using 10-period moving-average crossover dynamics to stay aligned with persistent directional strength.

α₄ Range Position

+15.2%/yr • Weight 2.5

Measures where the close sits within the daily high-low range to identify names finishing strong inside the session.

α₅ Mean Reversion

+14.8%/yr • 11/11 yrs

The contrarian sleeve of the model, buying oversold bounces when price stretches too far below recent equilibrium.
