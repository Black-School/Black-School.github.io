HOW ATLAS WORKS

STEP 01

📡 Scan the Universe

Every morning at 9:15 AM, ATLAS downloads fresh data for all 50 Nifty stocks — price, volume, momentum, volatility.

STEP 02

🧠 Run 5 Alpha Signals

Each stock gets scored by 5 uncorrelated quantitative factors — from volume-weighted momentum to Keltner channel breakouts. The signals are combined into a composite score.

STEP 03

🤖 AI Validation

Top-ranked stocks go through an LLM brain (Claude/Gemini) for fundamental analysis — news, earnings, sector sentiment. Only stocks that pass both quant AND AI filters get traded.

STEP 04

⚡ Execute & Protect

Orders fire automatically via Angel One API. Every position gets a trailing stop (+3% activation, 1.5% trail) and a 15-day max hold. Risk manager caps exposure at 18% per trade.

HOW THETA GOD WORKS

Options are like insurance contracts. Buyers pay a premium for protection. Theta God is the insurance company — it sells this "insurance" every day and keeps the premium when nothing happens. And most days? Nothing happens.

09:59 AM

📉 Sell ATM Straddle

Sell both a Call and a Put option at the same strike price (at-the-money). Collect the total premium upfront. If SENSEX doesn't move much — you keep it all.

ALL DAY

🛡️ Monitor with Stop-Losses

CE gets a 25% SL, PE gets 23%. Every 10 seconds the bot checks prices. If one leg moves against you, it exits that leg and trails the other to breakeven — making it risk-free.

03:06 PM

✅ Close & Collect

Square off everything. On a calm day, both options decay towards zero and you pocket ~70% of the premium. On a volatile day, the SL + trail system limits losses.

REPEAT

🔄 Every. Single. Day.

The edge compounds. 2,300+ trades over 10 years. 70% of them winners. The law of large numbers turns a small daily edge into massive wealth.
