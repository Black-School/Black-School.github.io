THE STRATEGY

How Orbinator Works

The first 15 minutes set the day's battlefield. Orbinator captures the opening range, then trades the breakout with strict risk management and a hybrid trailing stop.

STEP 01
Capture Opening Range
Record the HIGH and LOW of the first 15-minute candle (9:15–9:30). This is the day's reference range — the initial fight between buyers and sellers.
STEP 02
Wait for Breakout
Price closes above OR High → BUY. Price closes below OR Low → SHORT. Width filter rejects days with OR too wide (>0.75%) or too narrow (<0.05%).
STEP 03
Manage & Exit
SL = opposite end of OR. Target = 2× risk (1:2 R:R). Hybrid trail: lock breakeven at 1.5R, lock profits + candle-trail at 2R. Max 2 trades/day. Exit by 3:15 PM.
